Cross-team operations

  • Release - owner Eng Lead, weekly, lead-time 1 day. Escalation: Eng Lead, then CEO.
  • Quarterly planning - owner Head of Product, all teams, 60% bottom-up.
  • Monthly close - owner COO.
  • Roadmap intake (GTM to Product) - as-a-service queue, monthly.

How we work

  • Engineering: hybrid, anchor day Tuesday.
  • Ops & Finance: onsite Zurich.
  • Remote-EU engineers: async-first, decisions written down.

Where things live

  • Engineering -> engineering/
